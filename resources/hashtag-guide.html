CSG Notes & Red Account Compliance Reference

CSG Notes Hashtags (Renewal Opportunity — SEM_Notes__c)

License Hashtags (Required on ALL renewals)

Exactly one of the following must appear in every CSG Notes entry:

HashtagMeaningWhen to Use
#LicNoAttritNo license attrition expectedDefault when renewal is flat or growing
#LicPartialAttritPartial attrition expectedCustomer confirmed reducing some licenses
#LicFullAttritFull attrition expectedCustomer confirmed full churn

Signature Risk Hashtags (Required on Signature renewals)

HashtagMeaning
#SigRiskSignature renewal is at risk
#NoSigRiskSignature renewal is not at risk
#SigAttritSignature attrition confirmed

Red Account Reference

HashtagWhen Required
#RAWhen an active Red Account exists for the account. Must include a shortened URL to the RA record.

Agentforce Hashtags

HashtagMeaning
#AFAttritAgentforce attrition risk
#AfNoRiskAgentforce — no risk

Invalid Hashtags (Common Mistakes)

InvalidCorrect FormNotes
#NoAttrit#LicNoAttritMust specify license prefix
#NoSigAttrit#NoSigRiskUse risk vocabulary, not attrition
#PartialAttrit#LicPartialAttritMust specify license prefix
#FullAttrit#LicFullAttritMust specify license prefix
#NoRisk#NoSigRiskMust specify Sig prefix
#partialattrit#LicPartialAttritCase-sensitive

CSG Notes Format

Standard format for the SEM_Notes__c field on the renewal opportunity:

MM/DD/YYYY CSM_Name #LicHashtag #SigHashtag [#RA URL] [#AF hashtag]
Short narrative about account status and next steps.

Example:
06/02/2026 Benjamin Batista #LicNoAttrit #NoSigRisk Go-live blocked by Cimulate dependency; R&D prioritizing before July. KEM Memorial Day completed.
