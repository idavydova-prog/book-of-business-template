URL Shortener

Shorten Salesforce Org62 URLs for CSG Notes

Salesforce URL Shortener (sfdc.co)

https://sfdc.co
Internal Salesforce URL shortener — use this to create short links for Red Account records in CSG Notes

When referencing a Red Account in your CSG Notes with #RA, include a shortened URL so anyone reading the notes can quickly navigate to the RA record.

How to Use

1
Navigate to the Red Account record in Org62
2
Copy the full URL from your browser's address bar
3
Go to sfdc.co and paste the URL
4
Copy the shortened URL (e.g., sfdc.co/abc123)
5
Add it to your CSG Notes after the #RA hashtag

Examples

Full URL https://org62.lightning.force.com/lightning/r/Red_Account__c/a4Ved0000009ZQAEA2/view
Shortened sfdc.co/abc123
In CSG Notes 06/15/2026 CSM Name #LicNoAttrit #NoSigRisk #RA sfdc.co/abc123 Account status and next steps.

Why Shorten?

The SEM_Notes__c field has limited space and is read by multiple stakeholders. Full Org62 URLs are 80+ characters and clutter the notes. A shortened URL keeps the entry concise while maintaining direct access to the Red Account record.

Tip: Bookmark sfdc.co for quick access. You can also use it to shorten links to Opportunities, Accounts, or Cases when referencing them in notes or Slack messages.
