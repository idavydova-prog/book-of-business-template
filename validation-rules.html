Validation Rules

Comprehensive reference for all dashboard validations across CC, MC, Core, and Tableau

Overview

The Book of Business dashboards perform 12 types of validation and analysis on each account:

TypeColumn / LocationSeverityPurpose
Compliance CSG Compliance & RA Hashtag Compliance columns FAIL / PASS Validates required hashtags are present and correctly formatted
Freshness Colored dots next to CSG Compliance & RA columns Fresh / Aging / Stale Checks how recently CSG Notes and Red Account records were updated
Context Signal Analysis tab → Coaching Insights COACHING Flags mismatches between RA play type and CSG Notes assessment

1. CSG Notes Compliance (SEM_Notes__c on Renewal Opportunity)

Applies to: All clouds (CC, MC, Core, Tableau). Displayed in the "CSG Compliance" column. Accounts without an active renewal show "(No active renewal)" and are not validated.

Required: License Hashtag (exactly one must be present)

HashtagMeaningFails if...

#LicPartialAttritPartial attrition expected
#LicFullAttritFull attrition expected

Required: Signature Risk Hashtag (on Signature renewals)

HashtagMeaningFails if...
#SigRiskSignature at riskAccount has "Signature" in products/notes but none of these is present
#NoSigRiskNo Signature risk
#SigAttritSignature attrition expected
#NoSigAttritNo Signature attrition

Required: Red Account Reference (when RA exists)

HashtagWhen RequiredFails if...
#RAAccount has an active Red Account (any stage)#RA not found in CSG Notes when at least one active RA exists

2. Red Account Hashtag Compliance (ECOMM_Headline__c)

Applies to: All clouds. Displayed in the "RA Hashtag Compliance" column. Each Red Account's ECOMM Headline is checked for a valid play hashtag.

Valid Play Hashtags (at least one required in ECOMM Headline)

HashtagPlay TypeDescription
#SIGTRIALSignature TrialCustomer in a Signature trial period
#ARIAdoption Risk InvestmentARI-funded engagement to drive adoption
#SWAPSwap ProgramCustomer being migrated to a different product
#IMPLEMENTImplementationActive implementation in progress
#ADOPTAdoptionDriving adoption of underutilized (shelfware) licenses
#RFPRFP / CompetitiveOpen RFP or active competitive evaluation
#REVIVEReviveTop 100 Tableau attrition recovery

Invalid / Non-Standard (commonly seen but NOT valid)

Invalid TagWhy It Fails
#PRECAUTIONARYDescribes RA stage, not a play
#AgFStage4Internal process label, not an offensive play
#LOSTOutcome, not a play
#DCCAPProduct issue label, not a play
#nonactionableNot a recognized play hashtag
#KMODNot a recognized play hashtag
#MAPNot a recognized play hashtag
#januaryDate reference, not a play

Pass condition: At least one valid play hashtag found anywhere in the ECOMM Headline text (case-insensitive match). Fail condition: No valid play hashtag present.

5. Context Signals (RA Play vs CSG Notes Mismatch)

Applies to: All clouds. Displayed in the Analysis tab as orange coaching-style insights. NOT shown as inline compliance failures.

Rules

RA Play HashtagCSG Notes HashtagSignal SurfacedWhy This Matters
#RFP #LicNoAttrit RFP active but no attrition forecasted Competitive situations inherently carry attrition risk. CSM should confirm they've assessed and the customer will stay.
#SIGTRIAL #NoSigRisk Signature Trial active but no Signature risk noted A Signature Trial implies some uncertainty about the Signature tier. CSM should confirm the risk assessment.

Behavior

  • Only triggered when BOTH conditions are true (active RA with the play hashtag AND the specific CSG Notes hashtag)
  • Displayed as orange "coaching" insight cards in the Analysis tab
  • NOT shown as compliance failures in table columns (no red "FAIL" badge)
  • Intent: prompt managers to confirm the CSM's assessment is intentional during 1:1s
  • A CSM may legitimately have #LicNoAttrit with an #RFP RA — these are conversation starters, not errors

6. Utilization Thresholds

Applies to: All clouds (metric varies by cloud). Displayed in the utilization column as a dual-layer progress bar.

Metrics by Cloud

CloudPrimary MetricSource
Commerce CloudGMV (Gross Merchandise Value) or PPO (orders)EntitlementSchedule where name LIKE '%GMV%' or 'Commerce Cloud - Digital - PPO'
Marketing CloudSuper Messages (excluding SMS/MMS)EntitlementSchedule where name = 'Super Messages - excluding SMS/MMS'
CoreVaries (Data Services Credits, Einstein Requests, API Manager, etc.)EntitlementSchedule — highest-relevance active entitlement selected
TableauAnalytical Impressions (dashboard view renders)EntitlementSchedule where name = 'Analytical Impressions'

Color Thresholds (Projected End-of-Contract %)

ColorProjected %Risk LevelAction
Green< 50%Low utilization riskMonitor for adoption risk if very low (<30%)
Orange50% – 80%ModerateOn track or approaching limits
Red> 80%High utilization riskMay exceed contract limits — discuss early renewal or upsell

Summary Card Flag

Accounts below 30% utilization are counted in the "Low Util" summary card (labeled "GMV <30% Util" for CC, "Sends <30% Util" for MC, "Low Util" for Core, "Low Util" for Tableau). These represent adoption risk — customers not getting value from what they purchased.

Analysis Tab Insight

High-ACV accounts (≥$1M) with low utilization (<30%) are flagged as risk insights: "High-ACV accounts with low utilization — attrition risk if utilization doesn't improve."

Applies to: All clouds (CC, MC, Core, Tableau). Displayed as inline sparklines in the utilization column and a full-width trend chart in the Analysis tab.

Sparkline Color

Trend DirectionColorMeaning
Rising (>5pt increase from first to last)RedConsumption accelerating — may exceed contract
Falling (>5pt decrease from first to last)GreenUsage declining — possible adoption risk
Stable (within ±5pt)GraySteady state

Trends Chart (Analysis Tab)

A full-width SVG line chart at the bottom of the Analysis tab. Shows utilization trajectories for the top 10 accounts by Prior ACV. Each account gets a distinct color. Grid lines at 0%, 25%, 50%, 75%, 100%. Only renders when ≥2 history entries exist.
