Dashboard Benefits

Value delivered by the Book of Business dashboards across CC, MC, Core, and Tableau

Executive Summary

The Book of Business dashboards consolidate data from multiple Salesforce objects into a single-pane view per CSM manager. By automating compliance validation, utilization tracking, and risk identification across four clouds and multiple teams, the dashboards eliminate manual report-pulling and enable proactive, data-driven 1:1 conversations.

Benefits for CSM Managers

1:1 Prep in Seconds
Filter by CSM to see their full book — compliance gaps, stale notes, attrition risk — in one click. No more pulling 5 different Salesforce reports before each weekly sync.
Early Risk Detection
Freshness dots surface accounts where CSG Notes or Red Accounts haven't been updated in 3+ weeks. You see neglected accounts before they become escalations.
Compliance at a Glance
Instant pass/fail on every account's CSG Notes and RA hashtags. Identify training needs (who's below 70%?) and recognize high performers (who's at 100%?).
Context Signals for Coaching
Automated detection when a CSM's hashtag assessment may not match the account reality (e.g., #LicNoAttrit on an account with an active RFP). Conversation starters, not penalties.
Attrition Exposure Visibility
Total forecasted attrition is aggregated per manager with account-level drill-down. See which accounts carry the biggest renewal risk and whether CSMs are documenting it correctly.
Utilization-Driven Actions
Dual-layer progress bars show actual vs projected end-of-contract utilization. Immediately identify: (1) low adoption accounts needing engagement, (2) overages needing upsell conversations.
Utilization Trends Over Time
Inline sparklines show each account's consumption trajectory across refreshes. Spot accounts burning through entitlements faster than expected, or adoption stalling out — without waiting for QBR data.
CSM Workload Balance
Workload distribution panel shows accounts and ACV per CSM with imbalance flags. Instantly identify if one CSM is carrying 3x the load of their peers — a rebalancing conversation starter.
Data Currency at a Glance
"Last refreshed" timestamp in the header tells you immediately whether you're looking at today's data or last week's. No more guessing if the dashboard is stale before a 1:1.

Benefits for Individual CSMs

Self-Service Compliance Check
See exactly which accounts are missing hashtags, which are invalid, and what the correct tag should be — without waiting for a manager audit.
Clear Expectations
The validation rules are codified and consistent. No ambiguity about what "compliant" means — the same rules apply to every account across every cloud.
Renewal Calendar Awareness
Color-coded urgency (red = 60 days, orange = this quarter) makes it impossible to be surprised by an upcoming renewal. Sorted view by renewal date gives a natural priority order.
Red Account Tracking
All active RAs linked directly to Org62 records. Precautionary vs active distinction is visible at a glance. Resolved RAs are automatically excluded — no clutter.

Benefits for Leadership / Cross-Cloud Visibility

Unified View Across Clouds
Same dashboard structure, same compliance rules, same visual language across CC, MC, Core, and Tableau. Leaders can move between cloud dashboards without learning a new interface.
Standardized Process Compliance
PACE hashtag rules are validated identically across all clouds. No "our cloud does it differently" exceptions. One standard, one measurement.
Portfolio-Level Risk Aggregation
Analysis tab shows total ACV at risk, team-wide compliance rates, and utilization distribution. Instant answer to "how healthy is this portfolio?"
Coaching Pattern Recognition
Analysis tab surfaces systemic issues: which CSMs need hashtag training, which accounts are data-entry gaps, which RAs lack documentation. Patterns inform team-wide enablement.

Multi-Manager, Multi-Cloud Scale

Why One Dashboard Per Manager (Not One Giant Report)

Design ChoiceBenefit
Per-manager data.js isolationEach manager sees only their team's accounts — no information overload. Fast load times even with 48+ accounts.
Shared dashboard.js renderingFeature updates (like Context Signals) deploy to all managers across all four clouds with a single code change.
Consistent column structure across cloudsA manager who oversees multiple clouds (e.g., Avani Damboise on CC and Core) sees the same layout in both — only the utilization metric changes.
Cloud-specific home pagesEach of the four clouds has its own landing page with manager cards showing account/CSM counts — instant portfolio overview before drilling in.

Data Integrity Advantages

Single Source of Truth
All data pulled directly from Org62 via SOQL at refresh time. No spreadsheet copies, no manual entry, no stale exports. What you see is what's in Salesforce right now.
Resolved RA Auto-Exclusion
Red Accounts with Action_Plan_Status__c = 'Resolved' are automatically excluded. No manual cleanup needed — the dashboard always shows only actionable items.
Automated Compliance Logic
Invalid hashtag detection is programmatic and exhaustive — catches typos (#NoAttrit), wrong prefixes (#PartialAttrit), and wrong symbols (@LicNoAttrit) that humans miss in manual review.
Parent/Child Grouping
Multi-org customers (parent + child accounts) are visually grouped. Children follow their parent in all sort orders, preventing the same customer from appearing scattered across the list.
